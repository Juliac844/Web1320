About Anime

What is Anime and Why Do I Love It?

Anime refers to a style of animation that originated in Japan and has become popular worldwide. It encompasses a diverse range of genres and themes, targeting various age groups. Anime can be characterized by colorful artwork, vibrant characters, and fantastical themes. It is produced in various formats, including television series, films, OVAs (Original Video Animations), and manga (Japanese comic books or graphic novels). Anime covers a wide spectrum of genres, including action, romance, science fiction, fantasy, horror, slice of life, and more. It caters to audiences of all ages, from children to adults, and can be enjoyed by people with different tastes. Anime used to not be reconized by majority of people but in recent years the popularity skyrocketed, even the 2024 Superbowl Featured the well known JuJitsu Kaisen.

I loved anime since I was a child. My first ever anime i saw was Sailor Moon and i was enchanted by how colorful and magical it was. I remember forcing my mom to wake up in the middle of the night just to press the play button on my hanging tv to rewatch the same episode over and over again on my VCR. Ever since then, I have broaden my library of all different genres of anime. I genuinely believe when I'm old, I'll still be a fan.
